About the Solar Car Challenge

The Solar Car Challenge is the top project‑based STEM initiative helping motivate students in science, engineering, and alternative energy. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

The Challenge teaches high school students how to plan, design, engineer, build, and safely race roadworthy solar cars. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

The Solar Education Program provides hands‑on workshops, virtual learning videos, curriculum materials, and on‑site visits for high schools across the country. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

The end product of each two‑year education cycle is either a closed‑track event at Texas Motor Speedway or a cross‑country race allowing students to display their solar cars. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}
